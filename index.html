How It Works

  • Adjust lamps or sunlight to cast shadows

  • Align shadows into one continuous route

  • The boy walks only on shadows

  • Reach the guiding cat to finish the level

  • Unlock outfits and advance the dream story

Mechanics

How the game actually works: When you drag the lights around, the shadows shift in real-time Different light types (lamps, lanterns, sunbeams) Overlapping shadow puzzles Shadow-only navigation
